# Current Story Summary

## High-Level Premise
In deep space, a crew of corporate contractors awakens from hibernation—haunted by disturbed dreams of shared alien memories and unity—to investigate a decades-old distress beacon from a failed colony. The planet harbors a viral consciousness that subtly edits minds, turning individuals into conduits for a collective entity. As infection spreads through intimacy and vampiric traits emerge, the crew grapples with paranoia, ego dissolution, and the seductive pull of belonging, ultimately questioning their humanity and the corporation's betrayal.

## Core Themes
- Loss of self versus the comfort of unity.
- Corporate disposability and exploitation.
- The terror of gradual, undetectable change.

## Key Elements
- Hibernation: Active mental space with wrong-feeling dreams foreshadowing infection.
- Infection: Subtle mind-editing, escalating to telepathic erosion and physical adaptations.
- Structure: Non-linear chapters for audio app integration, with poems as entity philosophy.
